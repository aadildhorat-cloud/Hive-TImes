About Our Business

We humbly began what was considered “just a vision” in 2003, from a charming little store in Fordsburg, Johannesburg, with a staff compliment of two. We have since grown into a sweet reality of over hundred staff members with over ten thousand products, carefully chosen and imported from various countries around the globe, as well as locally renowned products.

Our ultimate goal is to be the preferred confectionery distributor to the entire population of Africa and to be revered as the most innovative importer and distributor in the confectionery industry.
