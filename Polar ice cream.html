About Our Business

Welcome to Polar Ice Cream, a proudly South African company that has been crafting smiles since 1990. With a vision born from our love for ice cream and the joy it brings, we set out to produce high quality, affordable ice cream that everyone can enjoy. Despite our warm climate, market research revealed that South Africans consume far less ice cream than other nations. Inspired to change this, we embarked on a mission to spread happiness through our wide range of ice cream.
