Compliance Certificates

All our work is certified and compliant with South African safety standards. We issue official Certificates of Compliance (CoC) for every electrical and plumbing job completed.

Electrical Certificate of Compliance (ECoC)

SANS 10142-1 Compliant
  • 📋 Wiring & connections from supply to control point
  • 📋 Distribution boards, circuit breakers & earthing
  • 📋 Wiring to outlets, switches & lighting
  • 📋 Earth leakage testing & safety verification
  • 📋 Valid for 2 years • Required for property transfers

Plumbing Certificate of Compliance (CoC)

SANS 10252 & 10254 Compliant
  • 📋 Proper geyser & hot water cylinder installation
  • 📋 Accurate water meter reading & stormwater management
  • 📋 No leaking pipes, taps or cross-connections
  • 📋 Correct fitting installation & isolating valve function
  • 📋 Issued by licensed plumber • Required for property sales

Why Compliance Certificates Matter

  • ✅ Legal Requirement: Required by law for property transfers, insurance claims, and municipal approvals.
  • ✅ Safety Assurance: Verifies installations meet national safety standards (SANS) to prevent fires, shocks, and water damage.
  • ✅ Property Value: Valid CoCs protect your investment and are often required by banks and insurers.
  • ✅ Peace of Mind: Our licensed technicians issue certificates with full accountability for work completed.
