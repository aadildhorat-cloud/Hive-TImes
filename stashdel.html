Integrated Supply Chain Excellence

Stashsell, a division of Haddag Holdings, is a South African wholesale distribution company supplying both general market products and imported goods. We provide verified, compliant, and reliable access to bulk items for resellers, businesses, and consumers across the country.

Our sister company, Stashdel, manages customs clearance, documentation, and transport, ensuring smooth movement. Through this integrated partnership, Stashsell delivers a transparent, efficient, and trusted supply chain within South Africa.

With our commitment to quality and compliance, we've established ourselves as a preferred partner for businesses seeking reliable wholesale distribution solutions in the South African market.
