About Stashsell

Integrated Supply Chain Excellence

Stashsell is a South African wholesale distribution company supplying both general market products and imported goods. As a division of Haddag Holdings, we deliver verified, compliant, and reliable access to bulk items for resellers, businesses, and consumers across the country.

Our sister company, Stashdel, manages customs clearance, documentation, and transport, ensuring smooth movement of goods. Through this integrated partnership, Stashsell delivers a transparent, efficient, and trusted supply chain within South Africa.

Our Core Values

Verification: Every product meets South African compliance standards

Reliability: Consistent delivery performance you can count on

Transparency: Clear pricing and tracking throughout the supply chain
