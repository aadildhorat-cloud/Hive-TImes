About Our Business

Who are we praise be to Allah (god) all mighty A family business, run on family values. Partnering with clients across South Africa supplying them with the highest quality meat from our own production and from our trusted partners in the meat industry. Relentless about quality, uncompromising about food safety and passionate about service – we strive to build lasting relationships and add value to our customers. Established in 2018 , with a passionate management team and expert staff. The butcher Block is a company built, sustainably from the inside out striving each day to be better. Owned by passionate South Africans who care for people, the environment and this great country we live in. We believe in supporting locally wherever possible without compromising quality. – we work with our suppliers to ensure better quality products and a sustainable supply chain.
