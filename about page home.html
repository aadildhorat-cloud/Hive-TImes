The world of online shopping is fractured. We saw an opportunity to bridge that gap, providing a single, powerful solution that gives businesses the best of both worlds.

What We Do

For Businesses

We provide a user-friendly platform to build and manage your online store. Our tools are intuitive, so you can focus on creating great products. We also offer robust analytics and marketing tools to help you find and connect with new customers.

For Shoppers

We make shopping an effortless and enjoyable experience. Our platform is designed for discovery, allowing you to browse, find, and purchase from unique businesses with just a few taps. We believe that shopping should be a part of your daily digital life, not a separate, clunky process.

Our Story

Hive Times was born from a simple observation: social media is great for discovery and impulse buys. Traditional e-commerce platforms offer control and data, but they can be complex and disconnected from where people spend their time online.

We are a team of passionate creators and entrepreneurs who understand the challenges of running a modern business. Our goal is to empower you with a beautiful, professional storefront that has the simple, social-first appeal that customers love.
